timeCapsule

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 28, 2026

Your memories never leave your device. This is not a marketing phrase — it is the technical reality of how timeCapsule works.

1. Overview

timeCapsule ("we", "the app") is a personal memory and life-milestone app for iOS. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles, how it is used, and what never leaves your device.

2. Data stored on your device

All of the following is stored exclusively on your iPhone and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Memory records (title, date, notes, location coordinates, place name, country)
  • Photos you add to memories (stored in the app's private sandbox)
  • App settings (language preference, theme selection, notification preferences)
  • Widget data (your most recent memory, displayed on your Home Screen)

We do not operate servers that store your personal data. We have no user accounts, no cloud sync, and no ability to access your memories.

3. Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services for specific features. Each is optional or limited in scope:

Mapbox (Location Search)

When you search for a place by name while adding a memory, the search query is sent to Mapbox's Geocoding API to return matching place names and coordinates. Mapbox's privacy policy is available at mapbox.com/legal/privacy.

  • Only the text you type in the search field is sent — not your GPS location.
  • If you use "Current Location" instead of searching, your GPS coordinates are determined by your device's on-board GPS and are never transmitted to any server.

Anthropic Claude (AI Title Suggestions — Optional)

When you choose to use AI-generated title suggestions while creating a memory, the app may send the following to Anthropic's API:

  • The place name and date of the memory
  • Photos you have selected for that memory (for visual context)

This feature requires your explicit opt-in and is clearly labeled in the app. If you do not use AI suggestions, nothing is sent to Anthropic. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Apple (StoreKit / In-App Purchases)

If you purchase optional themes (Globe Explorer Pack, Tech Pack, or Special Editions), the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive confirmation that a purchase was completed but do not receive any payment or personal information. Apple's privacy policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Device permissions

The app requests the following iOS permissions only when needed for specific features:

  • Photos: To let you add photos to your memories. Photos are copied into the app's private storage — your original Photos library is not modified.
  • Location (When In Use): To automatically detect your current location when adding a memory. Used only on request — the app does not track your location in the background.
  • Notifications: To deliver "On This Day" reminders about memories from past years. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not require a server.

5. Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect analytics or crash reports.
  • We do not display advertising.
  • We do not create or maintain user accounts.
  • We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not use tracking technologies (cookies, device fingerprinting, etc.).

6. Backup and export

The app includes a local backup feature that lets you export all your memories and photos as a ZIP file. This file is created on your device and shared using iOS's standard share sheet (AirDrop, iCloud Drive, etc.). We do not have access to your backup files.

7. Children's privacy

timeCapsule is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
